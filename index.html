About CTGP-64

CTGP-64 (Custom Grand Prix 64) is an unofficial custom mod for the classic Nintendo game, Mario Kart 64. When complete, the mod will add over 20 new tracks, including remixes from newer games and original creations.

Inspired by popular mods like MK64AU, CTGP-R, and CTGP-7, CTGP-64 is designed to bring many quality-of-life upgrades to the game. The project is currently in development with a target release date in 2026. No official demos or releases are available yet.
